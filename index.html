Who we are?

Grinta was founded by a group of entrepreneurs, pharmacists and doctors with a collective experience in the healthcare, digital transformation, and supply chain.

What we do?

Grinta is a Fintech-enabled B2B tech platform that modernizes the pharmaceutical supply chain by empowering independent pharmacists. The end-to-end platform offers a seamless and easy-to-use experience, giving access to the full spectrum of traceable pharmaceutical and medical products from multiple vendors in addition to providing fulfillment, demand planning, and inventory financing.

The company is working with all stakeholders across the value chain, manufacturers, distributors, wholesalers, and pharmacies, to build a data-driven, reliable, and efficient pharmaceutical supply chain.

Our Mission

We empower our clients to purchase their desired pharmaceuticals and supplies as well as providing them with tools to manage their working capital and inventory, through an easy to use and inclusive platform , empowering them to put their patients care first.

Our Vision

To become the main platform for pharmaceutical drugs, cosmetics and medical supplies and the key intermediary between pharmaceutical companies and pharmacies in Africa.
